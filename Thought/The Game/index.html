At a time when one ideology seems
to differ from the other.

Whether it is the one who considers itself
as a majority trying to prove a
baseless point suffers or the one
who considers itself to be a minority
that is trying to defend a baseless point suffers?

Ultimately, the one considering itself
in a minority has to suffer in an endless battle
in which both are parties are kept blindfolded
and have to acquiesce to play their role
in the game to cover the real thing.

In this game attackers and defenders
have a deal in which the mass
should always watch the game and
not the cult that is holding the stick.

- Pranay Patel
